Hostel Rules & Regulations

To ensure a harmonious and safe living environment, all residents are expected to adhere to the following rules and regulations.

General Conduct

  • Quiet Hours: Maintain silence between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM.
  • Visitors: Visitors are allowed only in common areas during designated hours (9:00 AM - 8:00 PM) and must be signed in at the reception. Overnight guests are strictly prohibited.
  • Cleanliness: Keep your rooms and common areas clean and tidy. Dispose of waste in designated bins.
  • Damage to Property: Residents will be held responsible for any damage caused to hostel property and will be charged for repairs.
  • Smoking & Alcohol: Smoking, consumption of alcohol, and illegal substances are strictly prohibited on hostel premises.
  • Pets: No pets are allowed inside the hostel.
  • Electricity & Water: Conserve electricity and water. Turn off lights and fans when leaving the room.
  • Security: Ensure your room is locked when you leave. Report any suspicious activity to the hostel warden.
  • Disputes: All disputes among residents should be reported to the warden for amicable resolution.
  • Emergencies: Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and procedures.

Mess & Canteen Rules

  • Adhere to the mess timings. No food is to be taken out of the mess hall without permission.
  • Maintain decorum in the mess and canteen areas.

Disciplinary Action

Violation of any of these rules may lead to disciplinary action, including fines, suspension, or expulsion from the hostel.

*The hostel administration reserves the right to amend these rules as necessary.
